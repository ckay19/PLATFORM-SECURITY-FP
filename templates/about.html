About SimpleBankApp

Our Mission

SimpleBankApp stands out for its comprehensive features and responsive support—offering secure, modern banking for everyone, on your terms and timeline.

We are committed to security, transparency, and user empowerment—especially when it comes to protecting your finances and making banking easy for all.

Our platform brings together the best of technology and banking expertise, ensuring seamless transactions, robust account management, and a user-friendly experience.

Features & Security

Key Features
  • Secure authentication

  • Instant transfers
  • Transaction history
  • Admin dashboards
  • PH location data
Security Measures
  • Password hashing
  • CSRF protection
  • Rate limiting
  • Session security
  • 2-step confirm
  • Admin approval
